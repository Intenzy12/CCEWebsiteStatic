Recreational Activity at Migrant Shelter
Volunteers will be assigned one day of the week based on their preferences and knowledge to offer recreational activities from 4:00pm to 6:00pm, to children and adults.

Some of the activities will fall into:
  1. Arts & Crafts Day
  2. Card & Board Games Day
  3. Dance/Exercise Day
  4. Outdoor Games
  5. Other
Responsibilities
  1. Plan out weekly activities.
  2. Obtain materials required for activities.
