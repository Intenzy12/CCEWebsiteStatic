Nuevo Amanecer
Students will first get to know how the Nuevo Amanecer organization works.

Later, on March 10th, students will have the opportunity to sponsor one of their students that is suffering of malnutrition.

Students will help in the organization of two birthday parties at the end of March and May for Nuevo Amanecer students. They will be key elements in the logistics of the party.

Finally, volunteers will have the opportunity to assist teens and grown-ups in the facilities to create products that help them make a living in the Taller de Imaginación.
Responsibilities
Commit to entire program
Arrive 10min before every meeting and/or event
Help out at parties/cleanup
Attend all meetings
Get to know the kids and their families.
