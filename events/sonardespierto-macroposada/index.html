MACROPOSADA
Registration Closed
We believe that a day of magic can give hope to children and awaken among our volunteers the genuine desire to contribute to our community. That is why Soñar Despierto organizes two events for approximately 600 children each year.
For our MacroPosada, which will take place on December 3, 2022, we are looking for 12 committed volunteers that will work directly with Soñar Despierto headquarters to support with the organization, logistics and promotion. (Volunteers are required to attend the MacroPosada event)

The meetings will take place every two weeks (days and hours to be defined as a group).
Responsibilities
  1. Organize the event (seek for venues, food, beverages, prizes, etc).
  2. Develop a budget and seek sponsors for the event.
  3. Design logistics of the event (transportation, operation of the event, etc)
  4. Design marketing strategy of the event.
  5. Gather and organize volunteers.
  6. Be present during the Dec 3rd event.
