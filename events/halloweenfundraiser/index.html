October Fundraising - Halloween Lunch Kits
Students will be divided into groups to make the lunch kits. Students will be crafting the bags by pasting stickers to decorate them, as well as baking brownies and doing party sandwiches. The bags will be done in the CCE office during study halls. The cooking and baking will take place at the house of one of the heads of the club.
Responsibilities
  • Organization, punctuality, no leaving early
Requirements
  • Knowing to cook or bake, and having a flexible schedule.
Positions
Depends on the amount of orders.
CSL Hours Depends on the amount of hours spent.
