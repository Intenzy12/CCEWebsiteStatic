Kermés de la Amistad e Inclusión with Unidos
Unidos is a Mexican organization that transforms lives through the interaction of people with and without disabilities through experiential activities to achieve a more aware, empathetic, and inclusive society.

The carnival is an event Unidos organizes to celebrate the day of friendship and inclusion. This event will take place on a Saturday afternoon. We will arrive after their thanks giving mass to support in the carnival in activities such as: receiving our friends from Unidos, handing out tickets, helping in the food stands, helping in the game stands, etc.
Responsibilities
  • Receiving/being with our friends from Unidos
  • Selling/handing out tickets
  • Helping out with the food
  • Helping out with the games & music
  • Helping out with the mass
  • You should always be willing to help out and follow the instructions that will be given to you in the capacitación
Requirements
  • Attend the Induction Meeting.
  • Arrive before 11:15am at ASFM to arrive all together at 12pm at Colegio Mexicano
  • Wear neon vest given by Unidos at all times
