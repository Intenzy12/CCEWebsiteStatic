Ruba Race 2022
We will start at 7:00 am and end at about 11:30 am. When you arrive at Isabela Gómez’s house, the first thing you will do is check in with her. Then we will drive to the race. The purpose of the activity as a whole is to raise money for Cardio Chavitos to pay for their surgeries. The volunteers will impact the race by not only helping the people participating in the event, but also by assisting the cardio chavitos team in the event. The volunteers will also impact the cause by helping raise money and make the Cardio Chavitos organization known to more people.
