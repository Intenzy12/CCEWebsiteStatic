Values Classes for HEB Grocery Packing Volunteers
ANSPAC stands for “Asociación Nacional Pro superación Personal”. They aim to encourage and help develop personal growth, as well as reinforcing relationships with the community and creating futuristic leaders. They promote this through courses where values and morals are taught with the sole purpose of advocating for positive changes in individuals and society.
This activity consists of once a week classes indifferent HEB locations, teaching a pre-designed “values.”
Responsibilities
  • Create weekly presentations for the topics
  • Attend preparation meetings once a week at lunch
  • Attend preparation meetings in ANSPAC once a month
  • Read the topic that is going to be discussed previous to the meeting and previous to the HEB visit
  • Prepare material and activities
