SAC POSADA
SAC members will be asked to organize the SAC POSADA which means they will have to prepare activities, food, songs to sing, gifts, and the logistics of the event. Then on December 3, 2o22 at 4:00pm members will be asked to arrive on time to the Iglesia Del Apóstol San Pablo where we will entertain the adult seniors with food, music, games, and gifts.

  1. People who will sing songs (all sac members)
  2. People who with the lotería game (4)
