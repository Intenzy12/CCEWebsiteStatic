Cáritas - Hambre Cero
Visit the food bank to learn what Cáritas does and how everything works.

Sort food and prepare food packages at the food bank.

Visit a dining room in Santa Catarina to help with different tasks (Cleaning, serving food, helping the visitors).

Go to the processing plant inside the Cáritas food bank to handle food by checking if it is edible, cutting the food, and packing it for donation.

Support with data collection from the communities that will support our planning the community visit.

Go to a community to help deliver the food and help distribute each bag to a selected family.

Attend all activities with good attitude and commitment.
