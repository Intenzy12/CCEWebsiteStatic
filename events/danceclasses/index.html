Teaching Dance Lessons
Become a dance instructor for girls in public schools to learn ballet, jazz, or hip hop! Once a week, visit a school to teach a warm up, across the floor, and class. Plan lessons, create choreographies, and work with the team to put together an end of a year recital.
Responsibilities
  • Visit schools consecutively
  • Plan lesson beforehand
  • Prepare an appropriate playlist
  • Instruct dancers
  • Attend meetings
  • Collaborate for the recital
Requirements
Attend all classes (1 or 2 depending on your frequency)
Positions
