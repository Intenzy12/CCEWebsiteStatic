DESIGN OF CRECIENDO JUNTOS FALL SEMESTER PROGRAM
We will redesign the Creciendo Juntos program, since we were not able to interact with the children that past two years due to the pandemic. Our objective is to offer enriching activities to both the children of the La Gran Familia orphanage and ASFM volunteers.

We are looking for 5 committed volunteers who are available to meet from September 11th to September 25th to create the fall activities plan, that will include a detailed description, budget, with operations and logistics for all activities in order to present to the director of La Gran Familia Orphanage and to recruit volunteers.
Responsibilities
  1. Organize the fall semester (seek for activities for the children, lesson ideas, crafts).
  2. Develop a budget and fundraise money required.
  3. Design logistics of the activities (operation of the activities, attendance, schedule, etc.)
  4. Gather and organize volunteers.
  5. Be present during the activities.
Requirements
Participate in the fall program (Two Saturdays a Month; Morning visits from 11.00am to 1:00pm)
