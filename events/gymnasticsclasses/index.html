Gymnastics Extra Curricular Program
We will be working with children from grades 1st-3rd and teaching them gymnastics basics throughout the whole year. Your responsibilities as a coach will involve guiding boys and girls through gymnastics-related activities. Involving skills including: carrying/guiding gymnasts through cartwheels, handstands, and walking on balance beams.
Responsibilities
  • Looking after, carrying and guiding a gymnastics class that consists of leading a warm up routine (basic stretches).
  • Being able to work with children, explain exercises and skills and have patience with the learning process
  • Being present at training sessions that you sign up for.
  • Reviewing and addressing any questions one day before the class begins.
  • Following the schedule/program of skills that gymnasts will be practicing.
  • Ensuring a safe environment to avoid any injuries.
Requirements
  • Attend all classes (1 or 2 depending on your frequency)
  • Some gymnastics experience
