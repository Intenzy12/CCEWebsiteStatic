VICO VALORES
Students will plan and teach weekly lessons at the Ramon Lopez Velarde Elementary School. Lessons will be focused on the value of the month determined by the SEP program.
Responsibilities
Attend an Induction Meeting: volunteers will be introduced to Soñar Despierto and the VicoValores program.

Attend One Training Meeting: Via Zoom, program directors will give a presentation to guide volunteers in the weekly lessons.

Attend Weekly Planning Meetings: Volunteers will get together to plan the weekly lessons.

Give the Weekly Lesson: Volunteers will go to the Ramón López Velarde school to teach their class..
