Vico Valores
Students will plan and teach weekly lessons at the Jaime Torres or Salvador Díaz elementary schools. Lessons will be focused on the value of the month determined by the SEP program.

Escuela Jaime Torres: Sierra Tereyal 646, INFONAVIT Lomas de Sta Catarina, 66359 Santa Catarina, N.L.

Escuela Salvador Díaz: Av. Manuel J. Clouthier SN, San Pedro 400, 66210 San Pedro Garza García, N.L.
Responsibilities
Attend an Induction Meeting: volunteers will be introduced to Soñar Despierto and the VicoValores program.

A ttend One Training Meeting: Via Zoom, program directors will give a presentation to guide volunteers in the weekly lessons.

Attend Weekly Planning Meetings @ ASFM: Volunteers will get together to plan the weekly lessons. (Meeting Date and Time to be determined)

Give the Weekly Lesson: Volunteers will go to the Jaime Torres or Salvador Díaz schools to teach their class.
