Due to the lack of funds from the Education System, Soñar Despierto will be supporting the 6 public elementary schools with the organization of their Christmas Posada.

Participating Schools:
Escuela Eduardo Caballero - 150 students
Escuela Ma. Teresa Rojas de Calderón - 150 students
Escuela Benito Juárez Ford 44 - 150 students
Escuela Ramón López Velarde - 300 students
Escuela La Gran Tenochtitlán - 300 students
Escuela José Eleuterio González - 300 students

Total students - 1,350

We are looking for 15 committed volunteers that will support us with the organization, logistics and promotion of all 6 events.

The meetings will take place every two weeks (days and hours to be defined as a group).
Responsibilities
  1. Organize the event (seek for venues, food, beverages, prizes, etc).
  2. Develop a budget and seek sponsors for the event.
  3. Design logistics of the event (transportation, operation of the event, etc)
  4. Design marketing strategy of the event.
  5. Gather and organize volunteers.
