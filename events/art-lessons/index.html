Art Lesson @ EC
Volunteers will teach 1hr30min dance lessons to elementary age students from the Eduardo Caballero School.

This activity will be held once a week from 4:00pm to 5:30pm, leaving from and returning to ASFM. Based on the availability of volunteers we will be defining a fixed day of the week.

Volunteers must support students to prepare 4 art pieces for an end of the year art exhibition in June.
Responsibilities
  1. Teach art knowledge and skills.
  2. Instill discipline, growth mindset and commitment in students.
  3. Design and plan weekly lessons.
  4. Support fundraising for art materials.
  5. Support with organization of end of the year exhibition.
