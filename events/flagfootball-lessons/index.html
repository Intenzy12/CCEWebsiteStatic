Flag Football
Volunteers will coach elementary aged kids the sport of flag football once a week for a one-hour session. They will prepare them to participate in a interschool tournament at the end of the school year.
Responsibilities
  1. Take and return training all materials to the school.
  2. Design flag football lessons.
  3. Help organize the tournament.
Requirements
  • Attend all planning meetings
  • Attend all coaching sessions
  • Flag Football Knowledge
