PowerChair

Powerchair is a community where we provide the necessary needs for physically diables people of all ages to enjoy the thrill of soccer. To do so accordingly, we require volunteers to assist the players (adjusting the chair components, helping them get in and out of the chair, setting up cones, helping the players with their training, etc.). Volunteers are required to commit from INITIAL DATE to FINAL DATE attending each Saturday for a 2-hour shift. (Volunteers are welcome to sign up for more than one shift).

Dates and Schedules

Every Sunday (8AM to 2PM)

Roles and Responsibilities of Volunteer

Provide any assistance necessary to the players by chair adjustments, training exercises and more. Also first and last shifts need to help take the chairs in and out of the containers and store them properly.

Volunteer Positions Available

Powerchair Pits:(Fixing and adjusting chairs like mechanics).
Powerchair Trainers (Helping on the field with player training).

CSL Hours

From 2 to 6 hours depending on the time present.
