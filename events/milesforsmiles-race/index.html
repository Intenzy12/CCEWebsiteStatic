Miles for Smiles Race Volunteer
Join us this Sunday for the annual Miles for Smiles Race by helping with the logistics of the race. As a volunteer you will be helping with many tasks, some of which might include handing out refreshments 0r medals, setting up the path of the race, documenting the race by taking pictures, cheering racers as superheroes while handing out water amongst many others! (Please not that if you sign up as a superhero you may receive preference as we have unlimited spots for superheros)
Responsibilities
Superheroes— Help with set-up and clean-up alongside giving waters or warm ups depending on assignment. Superheroes MUST bring their own costume to the race and ensure it has been approved by a miles executive.

Photographers— Help with set-up and clean-up alongside documenting the race. Photographers MUST bring their own device for taking pictures.

Start/Finish Line Overseers— Help with set-up and clean-up and either hand out medals, give directions, or hand out refreshments depending on assignment.
