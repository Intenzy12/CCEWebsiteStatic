Lost and Found Clean-up
Students will be sorting the different items in the MSHS lost and found into categories (water bottles, tupper wares, sweatshirts, jackets, pencil bags, etc). The sorting will be done in the Lost and Found storage area and then will be moved to the CCE storage room. All volunteers will participate in the November Bazaar for maintenance and security staff. The bazaar will take place on Saturday November 5th from 9am to 2pm in the MSHS cafeteria.
Responsibilities
  • Sort and organize, sell and manage items during the bazaar.
Requirements
  • All are welcomed.
Positions
15
CSL Hours 6 hours minimum (depends on participation).
