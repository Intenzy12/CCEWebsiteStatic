Micro Posada - Escuela José Eleuterio González
Due to the lack of funds from the Education System, Soñar Despierto will be organizing the Christmas posada for the José Eleuterio School with 350 students.

During the Christmas posada we will be hosting a fair (kermés) with 12 stands of games, a “Minute to Win It” show, and offering lunch to each student.
Responsibilities
  1. Mount fair (kermés).
  2. Manage a stand of the fair. (only 12)
  3. Host or Manage the Minute to Win It Show. (only 4)
  4. Play and entertain the kids responsibly and respectfully.
  5. Follow the protocols of the entertainment activities.
  6. Help with equal and responsible distribution of the food and drinks.
  7. Clean Up after event.
