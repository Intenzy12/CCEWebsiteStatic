Carrera Azul ARENA
“La carrera Azul” is organized by ARENA Asociación Regiomontana de Niños Autistas A.B.P with the objective of increasing awareness of Autism, which is commemorated every 2nd of April. Before the race, volunteers will have to attend an induction meeting, in which they will learn about Autism and ARENA’s cause, as well as attending the informational meeting, where they will learn the logistics of the race. Before the race, they will help to place the arch and sponsorship stands. During the race, they will be at the hydration points, giving out water to the runners.
Responsibilities
Everyone must attend on time to the meetings, and during the race to be active and give water to people.
